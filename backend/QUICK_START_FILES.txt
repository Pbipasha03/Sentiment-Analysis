## 📞 TROUBLESHOOTING

**"Models not trained"**
→ Click "Train Models Now" in app

**Low accuracy**
→ Use Logistic Regression (best model)

**CSV upload fails**
→ Ensure text in first column of CSV

**Streamlit not found**
→ `pip install streamlit==1.39.0`
